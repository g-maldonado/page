The Man Who Hates Peas - Short Film

A short film about a man who struggles everyday with a childhood trauma surrounding pees.

My Job

I am a Director and Co-Writer of "The Man Who Hates Peas"..

I had the ideia of creating a short film, for the final project for a course subject, about a guy who absolutely hates peas. The initial idea was pretty ambicious. I imagined, a guy named Diogo, going around various supermarkets and buying all the peas he could, just to incinerate all of them in the end. The concept was abandoned because most supermarkets dont allow you to film inside. The ideia evolved into one scene in a local store but got cut to due to the covid pandemic situation and some technical/schedulling problems.

In the final version you can see Diogo leaving his house empty handed and coming back with bags full of peas.
