Quarantine Simulator (Name Not Final)

A first person perspective story driven game with hints of horror where you play as a comedian amidst the coronavirus lockdown.

My job

I'm working as the Lead Programmer and Technical Artist of Quarantine Simulator. I'm responsible for the main game loop, the lighting work, the visual effects and cinematic cuts.

This game is my first attempt at using PBR materials and a realistic lighting setup and to achieve that I chose Unity Engine's HDRP (High Definition Render Pipeline)< as a base for the project.

The project is still in development.

Sinopsis

Bryan is a standup comedian who suffers schizophrenia. As the coronavirus outbreak starts, Bryan got himself locked up alone in his apartment. Not being able to see his psychiatry made his condition worse and changes his perception of the reality.
