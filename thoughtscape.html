Thoughtscape

A mobile 2D game about mental health inspired by Tetris and Kaboom!

My Job

I am the Producer, Game Designer and Lead Programmer of Thoughtscape.

The idea of Thoughtscape came from a desire to make a game about mental health.
I struggled with depression and anxiety for years and I wanted to share a bit of my experience. In 2020 I was getting the Ubisoft's Game Design Certification (Game Creator's Odyssey) and I needed a game design concept to the final assignment, so I submitted a mockup that became the origin of Thoughtcape. Is my first game and It was developed in 4 months.

Sinopsis

Sam is a young man who suffers depression and anxiety. Everyday is a struggle to Sam and when he takes a shower the loneliness and insecurity intensifies, despite the support of his friends and girlfriend. Catch his thoughts and help him go through the day.
